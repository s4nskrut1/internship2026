About us

About the studio.

ATLAS Studio is a small practice working on residential architecture, renovations, and selected hospitality interiors.

Studio story

One studio. Two scales.

The studio was established to work on projects where architecture and interiors should be resolved together. Early decisions about structure, openings, joinery, and materials are treated as part of the same conversation.

Most commissions are private homes. Some begin as new houses, while others are careful revisions of existing spaces. In both cases, the aim is to make rooms feel settled rather than styled.

Our studio

Small team.

ATLAS operates as a compact studio, allowing direct involvement from concept through drawing sets, site reviews, and final detailing. Collaboration with structural, lighting, and landscape consultants is integrated early.
